The Spirit of Leptis

Leptis Minor was an ancient maritime powerhouse on the Tunisian coast. Founded by the Libyans and chosen by the Carthaginians for its secure harbor, it became a thriving center of trade and culture.

Ceramics, olive oil, and art flowed through its port, linking Africa to the Roman Empire. It was a place where artisans thrived, creating objects that were both functional and beautiful.

Shaped by Nature

Today, we channel that heritage into Art Leptis. Every piece in our collection—whether it is a lamp sculpted from driftwood found on the shore, or a ceramic vessel—is handmade.

Like the ancient artisans of Leptis, we do not force the material. We listen to the wood, the stone, and the clay. We create objects that bring the warmth of the Mediterranean sun into your home.
